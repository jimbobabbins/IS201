Who is James Burchett?

What a loaded question that is. While it may seem to many that James Burchett is simply your average "Provo bro" attending Brigham Young University, there is much more to him than what meets the naked eye.

For example, upon looking at his appearance, you might not guess that he has an avid appreciation for Japanese automobiles. The photo to your right is one of James' personal favorites - the Honda Street. James' family also owns a 1983 Toyota Land Cruiser, which is his pride and joy. However, due to extreme inflation, James cannot afford to buy a Japanese car as they are extemely expensive at the moment. This is very sad for James.

Cars are not his only passion though. James also plays ice hockey, which is something to be expected from his time living in Canada. He also plays the guitar and various instruments, and has even recorded and produced a hit single, "Overthinking" by James Burchett (Featuring Jensen Diederich). You can give it a listen below!
